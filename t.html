1. Introduction

The purpose of online privacy policy is to maintain the privacy and protect the information of customers, employees, and all the stakeholders who are involved with Radian Finserv.

2. Objective

Regardless of geographic location, privacy policy is applicable to employees and all the stakeholders whose personal information is collected or processed with Radian Finserv. All the employees of Radian Finserv are expected to support the privacy policy to successfully meet the organizationâ€™s commitment towards data privacy. No third party shall access the personal information of employees and all the stakeholders.

3. Scope of the policy

The Data protection officer shall be responsible for the maintenance and accuracy of the policy. This policy shall be updated periodically with the recommendations provided by internal/external auditors and the same shall be updated by the data protection officer.

4. Policy compliance

On an annual basis compliance to the data privacy shall be reviewed by the compliance department. In case non-compliance is identified, the data protection officer shall review the reasons with a plan and report them to the compliance department. Depending on conclusions of the review, the policy shall be revised.

5. Data privacy principles

This policy explains the security practices for the appropriate use of personal information at Radian Finserv. As described in the policy transfer, collection of personal information shall be provided or governed as per applicable laws:

5.1 Notice

Notice shall be provided to the data subjects about how his/her personal information is disclosed, used, or retained.

5.2 Consent

Data subjects shall give their consent regarding how personal information about them is going to be disclosed, used, or retained.

5.3 Rights of Individuals

All the individuals have the right to control their information which includes access to data, erase the data, modify, transfer, and restrict and withdrawal of early consent that has been given by him/her.

5.4 Collection of Personal Information

Any personal information collected from data subjects with privacy notice and the purpose identified.
